Marry Me Chicken Pasta

4 servings

**Image Placeholder:** Add your photo here.

Ingredients

  • 680 g Uncooked Chicken Breast
  • 224 g Protein Plus Barilla Penne
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 0.5 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 2 cups low sodium chicken broth
  • 4 tbsp sundried tomatoes
  • 0.5 cup Plain Fat Free Fage greek yogurt
  • 4 tbsp philadelphia light cream cheese
  • 0.25 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 8 cloves garlic
  • 4 cups of spinach

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the high-protein pasta and cook until al dente. Reserve about 0.5 cup of the pasta water before draining. Drain the pasta and set aside.
  2. Season the chicken pieces with salt, pepper, and some oregano. Use a large pan or pot (or cook in two batches if necessary). Cook the chicken until browned and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the pan and set it aside.
  3. Reduce the heat to medium. In the same pan, add the minced garlic and sun-dried tomatoes and cook for about 30 seconds. Pour in the 2 cups of chicken broth to deglaze the pan.
  4. Stir in the remaining oregano, basil, and red pepper flakes. Allow the broth to simmer and slightly reduce for about 2 minutes. Reduce the heat to low, then whisk in the Greek yogurt and light cream cheese until the sauce is smooth. Stir in the Parmesan cheese until melted.
  5. Add the 4 cups of packed spinach to the sauce and stir until it wilts. Return the cooked chicken and the drained pasta to the pan. Toss everything to coat. If the sauce looks too thick, add a small splash of the reserved pasta water until it reaches the desired consistency.
