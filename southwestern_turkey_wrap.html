Southwestern Turkey Wrap

1 serving

Ingredients

  • 1 Xtreme Wellness Wrap
  • 56 g Hillshire Farm Oven Roasted Turkey Breast
  • 0.25 cup black beans
  • 3 tbsp good culture 2% cottage cheese
  • 2 tsp Pace Salsa
  • 0.5 tsp cumin
  • 0.5 cup baby spinach
  • 2 Tbsp Red Bell Pepper
  • 2 Tbsp Frozen Corn

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, combine the cottage cheese, salsa, and cumin. If you want, use an immersion blender or a food processor for about 10-15 seconds until the mixture is mostly smooth. (If you prefer a chunkier texture, just stir it well.)
  2. In a separate small bowl, take about one-third of your rinsed black beans and lightly mash them with a fork. Mix the remaining whole black beans and the corn and chopped bell pepper into the mashed portion.
  3. Lay the high-fiber tortilla flat on a clean surface. Spread the cottage cheese mixture evenly over the entire surface of the tortilla, leaving about a 1-inch border clear around the edges.
  4. Place the deli turkey slices on top of the cottage cheese spread, positioning them toward the center-bottom of the wrap.
  5. Spoon the black bean and corn mixture over the turkey.
  6. Pile the spinach or mixed greens on top of the other fillings. Wrap and enjoy!
