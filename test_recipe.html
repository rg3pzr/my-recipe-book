Homemade Chili

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lb lean ground beef (93/7)
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (15 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 oz) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp salt
