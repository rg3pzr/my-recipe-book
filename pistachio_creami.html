Pistachio-Vanilla Dream Ninja Creami

1 Serving

Ingredients

  • 180 g Plain Fage Fat Free Yogurt
  • 8 fl oz Fairlife Skim Milk
  • 1 Tbsp Honey
  • 7 g Sugar Free Vanilla Jello Pudding Mix
  • 0.5 tsp Pistachio Extract
  • 0.25 tsp Almond Extract
  • 0.25 tsp Xanthan Gum

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients (yogurt, milk, honey, pudding mix, extracts, and gum) in a bowl or blender and whisk/blend well until perfectly smooth.
  2. Pour into the Ninja Creami pint and freeze solid for 24 hours.
  3. Process on the "LITE ICE CREAM" setting.
  4. Add 1-2 Tbsp of extra Fairlife Skim Milk and run the "RE-SPIN" setting until creamy.
  5. Add toppings as you wish and enjoy!
