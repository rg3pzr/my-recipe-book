Ground Turkey Stuffed Peppers

4 servings

Ingredients

  • 4 yellow bell peppers
  • 454 g Trader Joe's 93/7 Ground Turkey
  • 0.5 cup uncooked quinoa
  • 0.5 cup red onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 14.5 oz can of diced tomatoes (no salt added)
  • 0.5 cup canned corn
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 0.5 tsp pepper
  • 0.25 cup reduced fat mexican cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 F. Cut the bell peppers in half lengthwise from stem to tip, and scoop out the seeds and membranes. Place them cut-side up in a $9\times13$ inch baking dish. Cook the $1/2$ cup of quinoa according to package directions (it should yield about 1.5 cups cooked).
  2. In a large non-stick skillet over medium-high heat, add the ground turkey. Cook, breaking it up with a spoon, until browned. Drain any residual liquid.
  3. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet. Cook until the onion is soft (about 3-5 minutes). Stir in the tomato paste, oregano, chili powder, and cumin. Cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Remove the skillet from the heat. Stir in the drained diced tomatoes, corn, and the cooked quinoa. Season the filling generously with salt and pepper.
  5. Spoon the meat and quinoa filling evenly into the pepper halves, mounding it slightly.
  6. Pour 1 cup of water into the bottom of the baking dish. This creates steam to help soften the peppers.
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the peppers are tender. Sprinkle the reduced-fat cheese over the tops of the stuffed peppers during the last 5 minutes of baking.
