Sheet Pan Honey Harissa Chicken and Garlic Onion Rice

4 servings

Ingredients

  • 680 g Uncooked Chicken Breast
  • 1 lb Raw Carrots
  • 1 medium red onion
  • 3 tbsp Trader Joe's Harissa Paste
  • 2 tbsp Honey
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 1 tsp avocado oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 0.25 tsp pepper
  • 1 cup uncooked long grain white rice
  • 2 cups low sodium chicken broth

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400 F. Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the harissa paste, honey, lemon juice, olive oil, garlic powder, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Cut carrots into chunks, and red onion into thick wedges.
  4. Add the chicken pieces, carrot chunks, and red onion wedges to the bowl. Toss until everything is evenly and thoroughly coated in the marinade.
  5. Spread the mixture onto the prepared baking sheet in a single layer.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes until the chicken is cooked and the carrots are tender and caramelized.
  7. While that is baking:
    1. In a medium saucepan, combine the uncooked rice, chicken broth, 1 tsp onion powder, 1 tsp garlic powder, dried parsley, and a heavy pinch of salt
    2. Boil: Bring the mixture to a rolling boil over high heat.
    3. Simmer: Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot tightly with a lid, and simmer for the time recommended on your rice package (15 minutes for white rice).
    4. Fluff: Remove from heat and let stand, covered, for 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork before serving with the chicken.
