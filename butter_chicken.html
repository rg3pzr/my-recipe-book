Cream-free Guilt-free "Butter" Chicken

4 Servings

Ingredients

  • 680 g Uncooked Chicken Breast
  • 0.5 cup Plain Fat Free Fage Greek Yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp deep ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 0.5 tsp turmeric
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp avocado oil
  • 0.5 medium yellow onion
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste (optional)
  • 8 oz canned tomato sauce
  • 0.5 cup low sodium chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp Argo corn starch
  • 0.5 cup unsweetened oat milk

Instructions

  1. Marinate and Cook the Chicken (15 mins): In a bowl, combine the diced chicken (~1 inch cubes), Greek yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, garam masala, turmeric, and salt. Mix well. Ideally, marinate for 15-20 minutes covered, then place in an air fryer for 8-10 minutes @ 375, flipping halfway through.
  2. Sauté Aromatics (5 mins): Heat 1 tsp of oil in a large pan or pot over medium-high heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until soft, about 3-4 minutes. Add ginger garlic paste.
  3. Build the Sauce (10 mins): Add the tomato paste to the pan and cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly. Pour in the canned tomato sauce and chicken broth. Bring the mixture to a simmer, then reduce heat to medium-low and let it cook for about 5 minutes.
  4. Thicken the Sauce (3 mins):
    1. Create Slurry: In a small bowl, whisk together 1 tbsp of cornstarch with 2 tbsp of the $1/2$ cup oat milk until completely smooth.
    2. Add Liquid: Stir in the remaining oat milk into the pan. Bring it to a gentle simmer.
    3. Thicken: Once simmering, stir in the cornstarch slurry and cook for 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly. The sauce should thicken immediately.
  5. Add Chicken & Finish (5 mins): Add the air-fried chicken pieces to the thickened sauce. Bring the sauce back to a gentle simmer.
