Ground Turkey Thai Basil Pad Kra Pao

4 servings

Ingredients

  • 454 g Trader Joe's 93/7 Ground Turkey
  • 0.5 medium red onion
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • 3 Thai Chili Peppers
  • 2 cups of fresh thai holy basil
  • 2 tsp of sesame oil
  • 3 tbsp low sodium soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 2 tsp honey
  • 2 tbsp chicken broth
  • 0.5 tsp white pepper

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, honey, water/broth, fish sauce (if using), and white pepper. Set aside.
  2. Heat the 2 tsp of oil in a large non-stick pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add the chopped onion, minced garlic, and sliced chilies. Saut√© for 2-3 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add the full 16 oz of ground turkey to the pan. Break it up with a spatula and cook until it is fully browned and no pink remains (about 7-9 minutes).
  4. Reduce the heat to medium-low. Pour the prepared sauce over the turkey and stir to coat evenly. Cook for 1 minute to allow the sauce to thicken slightly.
  5. Finish with Basil: Stir in the packed 2 cups of fresh basil and cook just until the leaves wilt (about 30 seconds). Remove the pan from the heat immediately to preserve the basil's flavor.
  6. Serve with rice and optional fried egg and enjoy!
